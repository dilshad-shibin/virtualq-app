User Manual- User App

  1. Home
  2. User Manual

1.Download Virtual Q App & Login With Your Mobile Number

2.Enter the OTP & get into the App

3.Select Your Location

4.Select the shop you want to book the slot for your order

5.Click on “new booking” to book the slot

6.Choose the time slot for pickup

7.Add item, quantity and unit(kg,box,litre etc)

8.Upload image if necessary by upload button

9.Click on “confirm booking” to place your order in the slot.

10.Booking information is available in “all booking” tab

11. Once booking is confirmed and ready for pickup, you will get a notification for pickup
